## ğŸ“ Overview

**BrainBot** is an AI-powered platform for learning and mastering board games, currently supporting **Chess** and **Checkers**. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, BrainBot provides an interactive and educational experience by leveraging artificial intelligence to:

- Play against bots or real players
- Get AI hints and suggestions during gameplay
- Analyze and reflect on past games
- Practice complex scenarios generated by AI
- Rewind games and receive feedback at every step

It's a complete training ground for sharpening your decision-making and game strategy.
